Sales Dashboard

The sales analysis dashboard provides comprehensive insights into profit trends, sales performance, and different categories and subcategoris and forcasting future sales , enabling data-driven decision-making. By visualizing key metrics and identifying patterns, the dashboard helps optimize sales strategies and uncover growth opportunities.


Questions that are answered:

  • What is the total sales revenue?
  • How has the sales changed over different time periods ?
  • How has the profit changed over different time periods ?
  • What is the average shipping days?
  • Which regions or countries are generating the most sales?
  • What are the projected sales for the next 15 days?

I took the following steps to create my analysis:

  • Collected data from Kaggle
  • Checking Datatypes
  • Performed data cleaning to handle missing values, duplicates, and inconsistencies.
  • Defined the layout and design of the dashboard, focusing on usability and clarity.
  • Selected appropriate visualizations (e.g., bar charts, line charts, pie charts) to represent different KPIs.
  • Built visualizations for each KPI, ensuring they were interactive and user-friendly.

Here are my key takeaways:

  • Regions in the west have highest sales
  • Office supplies, technology and furniture are the top 3 categories of sales.
  • Customers prefer cash on delivery payment method more.
  • Most of the customers prefer standard class shipping mode.
  • Phones and Chairs are widely ordered sub categories.
