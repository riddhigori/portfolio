PowerBi Dashboard
IPL Statistics Analysis

Discover insights from one of the world's most exciting cricket leagues with my IPL statistics dashboard. This project showcases comprehensive data analysis and visualization, providing a deep dive into match outcomes, player performances, and seasonal trends.


Questions that are answered:

  • Who are the top-performing players considering all the matches?
  • What is the distribution of match results (win by runs, win by wickets, ties, etc.)?
  • What are the most common dismissal types ?
  • Which cities have most number of matches played in?
  • Percentage of matches won based on toss decision?

I took the following steps to create my analysis:

  • Collected data from Kaggle
  • Performed data cleaning to handle missing values, duplicates, and inconsistencies.
  • Defined the layout and design of the dashboard, focusing on usability and clarity.
  • Selected appropriate visualizations (e.g., bar charts, line charts, pie charts) to represent different KPIs.
  • Built visualizations for each KPI, ensuring they were interactive and user-friendly.

Here are my key takeaways:

  • 62% of the total players are dismissed by a catch.
  • 64% of matches are won after the toss decision to field.
  • Mumbai has the highest number of matches played followed by kolkata.
  • AB Deviliers has won the highest number of player of the match awards
  • Death overs have most number of runs scored during first innings.
